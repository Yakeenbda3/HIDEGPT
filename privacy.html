Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 25, 2024

TL;DR: HideGPT does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything happens locally in your browser. We take your privacy seriously.

1. Introduction

Welcome to HideGPT ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how HideGPT handles information when you use our Chrome extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

HideGPT is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal information (name, email, address, phone number, etc.)
  • ChatGPT conversations or message content
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Authentication credentials or passwords
  • Location data or IP addresses
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health information
  • Any user-generated content
  • Analytics or usage statistics
  • Device information or system data

3. How HideGPT Works

HideGPT operates entirely within your browser:

  • Local Processing: All functionality runs locally on your device
  • No External Servers: We do not operate any servers that collect or process your data
  • No Third-Party Services: We do not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services
  • No Remote Code: All code is packaged within the extension with no external scripts

4. Data Storage

HideGPT uses Chrome's local storage API to save your preferences on your device only:

What We Store Locally:

  • Extension State: Whether Docs Mode is active or inactive
  • Response Style: Your selected style preference (indented, highlighted, comment, or grey block)
  • Document Title: Your custom document title (if changed from default)

Important Notes:

  • This data never leaves your device
  • This data is not accessible to us or any third party
  • You can clear this data anytime by uninstalling the extension or clearing your browser data

5. Permissions Explained

HideGPT requests the following permissions, which are used exclusively for the extension's core functionality:

Storage Permission

Why we need it: To save your preferences (Docs Mode state, response style, document title) locally on your device so they persist between browser sessions.

What we do with it: Store settings in Chrome's local storage. This data never leaves your device.

ActiveTab Permission

Why we need it: To detect when you're on chatgpt.com or chat.openai.com so the extension knows when to activate.

What we do with it: Check the current tab URL to enable/disable the extension appropriately.

Host Permissions (chatgpt.com, chat.openai.com)

Why we need it: To modify the ChatGPT interface and apply the Google Docs styling.

What we do with it: Inject CSS styles and JavaScript to transform the visual interface. All processing happens locally in your browser.

6. Third-Party Services

HideGPT does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or external APIs. Your usage of the extension is completely private.

ChatGPT Integration

HideGPT modifies the visual appearance of ChatGPT but does not:

  • Intercept or modify your ChatGPT conversations
  • Send any data to our servers
  • Change how ChatGPT processes your messages

Your interactions with ChatGPT remain subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

7. Data Security

Since HideGPT does not collect or transmit any data, there is no data to secure on external servers. All settings are stored locally using Chrome's secure storage APIs.

8. Children's Privacy

HideGPT does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any data, the extension can be used by individuals of all ages.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

If we make material changes to how we handle user data (e.g., if we ever start collecting data), we will notify users through the Chrome Web Store listing and require explicit consent.

10. Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's no personal information for us to access, modify, or delete. You have full control over the extension:

  • Disable: Toggle the extension off anytime using the browser toolbar
  • Uninstall: Remove the extension completely from Chrome
  • Clear Data: Clear your browser's local storage to remove saved preferences

11. Compliance

HideGPT is designed to comply with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable privacy regulations

12. Open Source

HideGPT's code can be reviewed by anyone. You can inspect the extension's source code to verify our privacy claims:

  • View the unpacked extension files in Chrome
  • Review our code on GitHub (if applicable)
  • Verify that no external network requests are made

14. Consent

By installing and using HideGPT, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

Summary: HideGPT is a privacy-first extension. We don't collect your data, we don't track you, and we don't sell anything to third parties. Your ChatGPT usage remains completely private.
